We are Prindle Engineering, a group comprised of 5 high school seniors who are currently enrolled in Project Lead the Way's 4th year Engineering Design and Development course. The objective of the course this year is to find a local problem and present an efficient solution that could solve or alleviate the issue both locally and globally. Each member of our group has their own specific role to fulfill. Below you can see a brief description of the roles and responsibilities of each member of the group.

A description of our chosen problem can be found here. Please check it out!
