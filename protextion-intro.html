Introducing... Protextion

What is it?

Ever bump into anything while using a cell phone? As its title suggests, Protextion is a new device designed to protect the crowd from these types of situations. In short, it awares cell phone users of any upcoming obstacles in their path that they may not notice due to being distracted.

Did you know?

Over 5,000 people per year hurt themselves or those around them because they fail to pay attention when they walk while using their cell phone. This is known as distracted walking, and it is becoming progressively worse every year.Prindle Engineering's goal is to prevent this problem from climbing any higher.
